• Authentically Sri Lankan • Pure Cotton • Handmade Elegance


Sinhela Bathik is a proudly Sri Lankan brand committed to preserving and promoting the timeless art of Batik, a craft deeply rooted in our cultural identity. Every garment is meticulously handcrafted using 100% pure cotton fabrics, ensuring comfort, quality, and authenticity. What makes us unique is that each design is one-of-a-kind, with only a single piece available per style, reflecting exclusivity and individuality for every customer. Our talented artisans blend traditional techniques with modern aesthetics, creating elegant apparel that resonates with both heritage and contemporary fashion. By choosing Sinhela Bathik, you are not only embracing authentic Sri Lankan craftsmanship but also supporting sustainable practices and empowering local artisans.
